About Taptune

Rhythm Recording

How It Works

Tap Tune’s Rhythm Recording takes the User’s input and synchronizes it to a standard tempo of 60bpm.
This rhythm pattern is compared to a song’s rhythm analysis, also synchronized to 60bpm, to find a set of beats in the song that match the users input based on timing. A song has four different kinds of analysis data stored for it: onset, peak, percussion, and harmonics. Based on how many matched beats are found and the error range of each match, the program makes a decision if the song contains a matching rhythmic pattern.

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start Recording" button.
  2. When you are ready, start tapping anywhere on the screen to record the rhythm of the song you are thinking of.
  3. If you feel as if you messed up on the input, feel free to reset the recording with the reset button.
  4. Once you tapped around 8-20 beats, click "Stop"
  5. Listen back to the rhythm that you recorded and if you think it's accurate, "Submit" your recording.
  6. Input any filters that you think would help with our search.
  7. Wait for the website to find your song and you should find our top main result based on your input recording and filters.

Melody Recording

How It Works

TapTune's Melody Recording feature uses multiple audio/song fingerprinting services to find the Users song based on a recording of them humming, singing, or recording a live source.
To ensure further accuracy , Tap Tune uses Speech Recognition to record the lyrics sung by a user, a search is then made to find songs using the recognized lyrics. The resulting songs found by the lyric recognition are then compared to the resulting song list from the fingerprinting services for more accurate results.

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start Recording" button.
  2. When you are ready, start singing and humming to the song you're thinking of
  3. If you feel as if you messed up on the input, feel free to reset the recording with the reset button.
  4. Once you sing or hum at least 10 seconds, click "Stop"
  5. Listen back to your singing/humming that you recorded and if you think it's accurate, "Submit" your recording.
  6. Wait for the website to find your song and you should find our top main result based on your input recording.
