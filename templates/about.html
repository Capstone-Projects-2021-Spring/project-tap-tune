Rhythm Recording

TapTune's Rhythm Recording feature takes your input of beats to a song's rhythm and any filters that you can remember to describe the song. It will then use a search algorithm after analyzing your input values to find the song that you are thinking of and display what we found.

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start Recording" button.
  2. When you are ready, start tapping anywhere on the screen to record the rhythm of the song you are thinking of.
  3. If you feel as if you messed up on the input, feel free to reset the recording with the reset button.
  4. Once you tapped around 8-20 beats, click "Stop"

  6. Input any filters that you think would help with our search.
  7. Wait for the website to find your song and you should find our top main result based on your input recording and filters.

Melody Recording

TapTune's Melody Recording feature takes your singing/humming of a song you can remember. It will then use a search algorithm after analyzing your input values to find the song that you are thinking of and display what we found.

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start Recording" button.
  2. When you are ready, start singing and humming to the song you're thinking of
